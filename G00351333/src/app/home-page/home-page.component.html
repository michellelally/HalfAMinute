How to play

Gather as many people as you can to play and divide everyone up into teams.
The aim of the game is to explain the 5 random words that are displayed on the screen
However, you have only got 30 seconds to do so!
Pick a person to begin and press the 'Start Game' button
Once this is cliked, a timer will begin and alert you once your 30 seconds are up
So then you give it your best go at describing each word on the screen
If you don't know the word, you can always skip it and come back to it

THE DESCRIBER CAN NOT:

    • Say the actual names on the card (but may say words like "and", "the", "on", etc)
      Use "sounds like" or "rhymes with" tactics.
      Use letters of the alphabet (may not say "starts with an A" or spell it out)
      Point to anything
